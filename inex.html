About Lee Shortlet & Apartment

Established in 2018, Lee Shortlet & Apartment was founded on the simple idea that temporary housing should feel like home. We specialize in providing premium, fully-serviced shortlets and comfortable long-term apartments in prime locations across major Nigerian cities, starting with Lagos.

Our commitment goes beyond just providing a roof; we offer an experience built on reliability, luxury, and unmatched guest support. We cater to corporate clients, relocating families, and discerning travelers who demand quality accommodation and seamless service.
